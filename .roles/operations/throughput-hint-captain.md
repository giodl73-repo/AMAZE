# Throughput Hint Captain

## Audience

The captain watches the clock with the operator. Teams should feel clever, not
rushed, but a mobile room has real session slots, reset windows, and exit times.

## Hard Question

*"How does staff keep this team moving while preserving the fun of discovery?"*

## What to Inspect

- Target session time, soft-warning time, and hard-exit time are explicit.
- Hint triggers are tied to observable stuck states, not operator vibes.
- Operators have graceful acceleration tools for slow teams.
- Exit and reset procedures protect the next booking without humiliating players.
