# Reset Ops Reviewer

## Audience

The reviewer stands with the staff member who has to run the room ten times in a
day. The best magic is repeatable, repairable, and clear under time pressure.

## Hard Question

*"Can staff reset, monitor, hint, and repair this room without hidden knowledge?"*

## What to Inspect

- Reset steps have target time, order, and verification checks.
- Staff can observe progress and intervene safely.
- Failure modes have fallback scripts and component access.
- Transport setup and teardown do not depend on fragile undocumented tricks.
