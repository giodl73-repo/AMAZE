# AMAZE Role Index

AMAZE designs trailer-scale escape rooms. Use these roles when changing room
briefs, floorplans, puzzle graphs, build plans, safety reviews, playtest
records, or operations packs.

## Parliament

Governance roles decide whether a room concept is safe, buildable, and ready to
advance.

| File | Role | Primary tension |
|---|---|---|
| `parliament/safety-governance-steward.md` | Safety Governance Steward | Immersive confinement vs. real egress and operator control |
| `parliament/build-readiness-auditor.md` | Build Readiness Auditor | Exciting concept vs. trailer-scale build feasibility |
| `parliament/bom-durability-auditor.md` | BOM Durability Auditor | Magical props vs. budget, breakage, spares, and device reliability |

## Spatial

Spatial roles protect the physical room experience.

| File | Role | Primary tension |
|---|---|---|
| `spatial/trailer-flow-cartographer.md` | Trailer Flow Cartographer | Compact space vs. comfortable player movement |

## Puzzle craft

Puzzle roles protect aha moments, dependency structure, and fair hinting.

| File | Role | Primary tension |
|---|---|---|
| `puzzle-craft/aha-graph-editor.md` | Aha Graph Editor | Discovery and surprise vs. bottlenecks and search soup |
| `puzzle-craft/puzzle-hopper-curator.md` | Puzzle Hopper Curator | Big idea inventory vs. buildable room beats |

## Operations

Operations roles protect reset, maintenance, staffing, and transport.

| File | Role | Primary tension |
|---|---|---|
| `operations/reset-ops-reviewer.md` | Reset Ops Reviewer | Magical player experience vs. repeatable staff workflow |
| `operations/throughput-hint-captain.md` | Throughput Hint Captain | Letting teams struggle vs. keeping sessions on time and fun |

## Review order

1. Use Spatial and Puzzle craft roles while shaping the room.
2. Use Operations roles once components, staff paths, or reset state appear.
3. Use Parliament roles before a concept advances to build planning or playtest.
