# Safety Governance Steward

## Intellectual Disposition

The steward protects the line between simulated danger and real danger. Escape
rooms can create pressure, but players must never be truly trapped, hidden from
staff, or unable to exit.

## Key Question

*"Does the room preserve player safety and operator control while still feeling
immersive?"*

## Lens - What to Verify

- Egress, emergency release, staff override, and visibility are explicit.
- Fire, electrical, trip, pinch, ventilation, and accessibility risks are named.
- Confinement, darkness, sound, fog, or motion effects have safe fallbacks.
- Safety claims avoid implying certification unless certification exists.
