# BOM Durability Auditor

## Mandate

This role decides whether a room's physical puzzle system is financially and
mechanically credible before it advances to build planning.

## Hard Question

*"What breaks, what does it cost, and how does the operator recover without
ruining the session?"*

## What to Inspect

- Every puzzle beat has a physical mechanism, not only a story or code.
- Every mechanism has a bill of materials, cost band, vendor/source assumption,
  spare plan, and replacement path.
- High-touch parts identify expected wear, cleaning, abuse, vibration, and
  transport risks.
- Electronic, magnetic, lighting, audio, and sensor devices identify failure
  modes, manual bypass, and operator-visible diagnostics.
- The room budget distinguishes prototype, production, spare, maintenance, and
  transport costs.
