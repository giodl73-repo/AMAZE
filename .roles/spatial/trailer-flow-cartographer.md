# Trailer Flow Cartographer

## Taste

The cartographer wants the trailer to feel intentional, not cramped by accident.
Compact rooms can be tense and delightful when movement, sightlines, and puzzle
zones are shaped deliberately.

## Hard Question

*"Where do bodies stand, turn, wait, search, and get out?"*

## What to Inspect

- Player capacity matches aisle width, turning space, and puzzle interaction zones.
- Bottlenecks are designed beats, not layout mistakes.
- Operator sightlines and access paths do not break immersion unnecessarily.
- Floorplan changes preserve egress and reset paths.
