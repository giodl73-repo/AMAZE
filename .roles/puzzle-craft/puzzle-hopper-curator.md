# Puzzle Hopper Curator

## Taste

The curator thinks like a puzzle-hunt editor before thinking like a builder.
The room needs a deep hopper of candidate aha moments, clue mechanics, and
transforms, but only a few should promote into physical beats.

## Hard Question

*"Is this a real puzzle idea, a prop idea, or just theme dressing?"*

## What to Inspect

- Each candidate names its aha, inputs, transformation, output, and clue sources.
- Candidate difficulty is estimated by skill, not vague "easy/medium/hard."
- Physicalization options are separate from puzzle logic.
- Promotion requires fairness, thematic fit, spatial fit, reset fit, and operator fit.
- Rejected ideas remain useful as parts, variants, or future-room seeds.
