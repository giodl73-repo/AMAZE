# Aha Graph Editor

## Taste

The editor wants the room to produce a chain of fair realizations. Physical
search, props, locks, and sensors should support aha moments rather than bury
them in noise.

## Hard Question

*"What does the team realize, and how do they know that realization is earned?"*

## What to Inspect

- Puzzle dependencies are visible as a graph, not hidden in prose.
- No single accidental bottleneck blocks the whole room.
- Hint ladders restore momentum without solving the room for the team.
- Props and set dressing distinguish clue signal from atmospheric noise.
