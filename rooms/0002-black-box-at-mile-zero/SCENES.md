# The Black Box at Mile Zero - Scenes

## Party assumptions

| Team archetype | Personas/roles | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Amazing team | mapper, handler, notetaker, skeptic | Should discover the central object contract quickly and ask for deeper mystery. |
| Confused team | searcher, hesitant handler, quiet observer | Must be pulled back to the box without shame. |
| Fighting team | dominant handler, ignored reader, frustrated solver | Central object must not let one person monopolize the whole room. |
| Chaotic team | prop toucher, joke-maker, speed-reader | No-force affordances and reset-safe props are critical. |
| Accessibility-varied team | seated player, audio-sensitive player, companion handler | Required beats must work without crawling, climbing, or audio-only clues. |
| Operator stress team | late arrival, tired group, one player disengaged | Operator must keep the run safe, legible, and on time. |

## Team archetype probes

| Team archetype | Team promise/risk | Scene/beat probe | Observable signal | Operator response | Design revision |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Amazing team | May solve too fast and feel thin. | P1-P3 | They explain the box-state rule before hint time. | Let them chase optional interpretation on evidence backs. | Add optional depth if target total is too low. |
| Confused team | May search instead of model the object. | P1-P2 | They handle tags randomly and ignore window changes. | Prompt matching marks between board and box. | Strengthen affordances and reduce visual noise. |

| Operator stress team | Timing and safety must hold under messy conditions. | Full run | Operator can identify state, hint, bypass, and reset. | Follow OPS ladder. | Simplify state if operator cannot track it. |

## Operator interventions

| Trigger | Intervention | Fiction-preserving phrasing | Escalation |
|---|---|---|---|
| Random search | Point to evidence marks | "The chain of custody only logged what matters." | identify first matching mark |
| Box monopolized | Assign roles | "Protocol needs a caller, a checker, and one handler." | ask handler to step back for one clue |
| Audio block | Move to transcript | "The recorder was transcribed for court." | reveal one indexed word |
| Force on latches | Stop unsafe action | "If it resists, the recorder is protecting the evidence." | manual bypass and post-run inspection |
| Hard-exit minus 4 | Accelerate final state | "Dispatch just found the last chain-of-custody note." | set final latch path |
