# Stormglass Station Operations

## Staffing

| Role | Responsibility | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Primary operator | briefing, hints, safety, final release | one staff member can run seed prototype |
| Reset operator | reset/repair support during high-volume events | optional but recommended for first tests |
| Builder on call | handles socket/dial fixes | required until console is durable |

## Run clock

| Phase | Target min | Operator focus | Acceleration |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Brief/safety | 3 | exit, no-force, roles | shorten story, keep safety |
| P1 role frame | 4 | confirm three instrument jobs | name pressure/wind/tide |
| P2-P4 instruments | 19 | watch parallel progress and crowding | first-card/detent/false-tide hint |
| P5 final | 7 | enforce caller/checker/handler | staff proof/manual route |
| Payoff/exit | 3 | beacon wrap and exit | immediate route card |
| Buffer | 9 | variance, photos, reset prep | hard exit script |

## Hint protocol

| Level | Use when | Style | Example |
|---|---|---|---|
| Nudge | progress stalls but team has right object | in-fiction redirect | "The station log asks for readings, not souvenirs." |
| Point | wrong subsystem for 2+ minutes | physical pointer | point to vane arrow or tide float |
| Demonstrate | slow max approaching | show one false case | "This tide does not align; look for one that does." |
| Accelerate | safety/clock pressure | partial answer or staff proof | staff proof cartridge after team explains solve |
| Bypass | component failure | manual route/reveal | route card and operator beacon script |

## NPC and operator characters

| NPC/voice | Function | Trigger | Delivery mode | Sample line | Limits | Reset/ops burden |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Watch Officer | onboarding, role framing, instrument hints | P1 job confusion, P2-P4 stalls | live operator voice or station speaker | "The station log asks for readings, not souvenirs." | does not name all readings unless accelerating | watch script and role cards reset |
| Harbor Beacon | final proof and payoff voice | P5 route call, socket jam, button forcing | beacon light/audio plus operator line | "The transmitter is locked until the instruments agree." | cannot transmit without proof sockets or staff-verified proof | manual route card and beacon script |

## Multi-room operator rotation

| Scope | Coverage model | Max rooms | Safe when | Unsafe when | Handoff signal | Dedicated staff trigger |
|---|---|---:|---|---|---|---|
| Weather-station trailer pair | roving operator with proof-console camera | 2 | both rooms are before beacon finale and cartridge states are visible | console jam, glare support, final route call, or reset | instrument-state board and beacon status light | manual route release, access support, or player force |
| Escape-room building cluster | central desk plus hall runner | 3 | rooms are staggered and each proof console has camera/audio | overlapping beacon finales or no runner | desk log with pressure/wind/tide states | stuck shutter, egress issue, or player distress |

## Reset checklist

| Step | Action | Verification | Target min |
|---|---|---|---:|
| 1 | clear team and keep aisle open | door clear, no props in egress | 1 |
| 2 | reset P1 log and role cards | cards in order, log closed | 1 |
| 3 | reset P2 dial and pressure cartridge | pointer home, cartridge hidden | 1 |
| 4 | reset P3 wind rack | vane home, all cards counted | 2 |
| 5 | reset P4 tide gauge | slider home, overlay pouch, cartridge hidden | 2 |
| 6 | reset P5 console/signal | cartridges removed, shutter closed, buttons home | 2 |
| 7 | final safety sweep | aisle clear, reset photo matches | 1 |

## Failure modes

| Failure | Detection | Player-facing script | Staff action | Post-run fix |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Missing role card | count mismatch | "The watch log has a spare duty card." | use duplicate card | replace print |
| Barometer pointer drift | dial mark mismatch | "The old needle needs station calibration." | give pressure cartridge after explanation | tighten pointer |
| Wind card missing | rack count short | "A duplicate reading is in the station pouch." | swap duplicate card set | add tether or magnet |
| Tide glare | players avoid overlay | "Use the inspection light." | turn on work light/printed tide card | matte overlay |
| Console socket jam | cartridge will not seat | "The station accepts the verified spare." | staff proof/manual route | sand/bevel socket |
| Button cap loose | cap comes off | "The beacon can be called manually." | verbal beacon and spare cap | replace cap |
