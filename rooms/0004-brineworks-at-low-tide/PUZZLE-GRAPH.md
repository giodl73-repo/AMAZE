# The Brineworks at Low Tide - Puzzle Graph

This is an original undersea-comedy food-service room. It must stay physical:
players should sort, snap, route, weigh, ring, seat, and reset objects instead of
reading a menu worksheet.

## Phase mix

| Role | Phase | Base skills | Why it belongs in this room |
|---|---|---|---|
| Signature | mechanical state | setting, sequencing, state validation | The pressure griddle gives the room one central object that accepts proofs. |
| Support | classification/indexing | color/shape sorting, order building | A lunch rush is naturally about making the right order from visible parts. |
| Support | path/flow | routing, valves, cause/effect | Undersea current pipes make trailer walls useful and physical. |

## Bottleneck check

- Required bottlenecks: final pressure griddle only.

- Final convergence: all three service proofs restart the griddle.
