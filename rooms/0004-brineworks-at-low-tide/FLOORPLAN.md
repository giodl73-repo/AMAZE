## Flow

| Moment | Player movement | Gathering point | Crowding risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Intro | Team enters as emergency canteen crew. | entry / shift clock | door bottleneck | first clue away from threshold |
| Service split | Players divide between order rail, pipe board, register. | station fronts | everyone at bins | three separate proof jobs |
| Proof check | Team deposits ticket, gear, shell tag. | proof rail | loose proofs in pockets | labeled homes outside path |
| Griddle restart | Team seats proofs at pressure griddle. | griddle face | one player monopolizes sockets | caller/checker/handler protocol |
| Exit | Open token releases. | griddle then door | celebration blocks egress | open token away from door swing |

## Transport and mounting notes

| Item/zone | Mounting assumption | Travel risk | Secure/stow plan |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pressure griddle | secured counter/cabinet | gauge/socket shift | transport lock |
| Ingredient bins | mounted bins or lidded tray | tile loss | lidded reset tray |
| Pipe board | wall-mounted modular board | elbow drift | panel lock and state photo |
| Clam register | bolted counter prop | hinge/pinch drift | latch or foam spacer |
| Proof rail | shallow mounted rail | token home shift | removable cover |

## Open spatial risks

- Order bins can become a crowd magnet.
- Pipe board must be low and large enough for seated participation.
- Pressure griddle must not invite leaning, force, heat assumptions, or egress blockage.
- Proof rail must not become a prop dump.
