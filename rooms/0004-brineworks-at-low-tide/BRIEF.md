## Story spine

The Brineworks feeds the reef every noon tide. Today the tide is early, the
ingredient bins are singing in the wrong order, the current pipes are crossed,
and the old pressure griddle refuses to open until the crew proves the order,
the current, and the bell.

The players are handed a waterproof shift card: fix the lunch rush before the
reef dome loses pressure. The room should feel like running a ridiculous physical
kitchen, not solving a paper menu.
