## Bottleneck check

- Required bottlenecks: act relic milestones and final orrery convergence.
- Accidental bottlenecks: drawer search soup, color/glare, memory overload, fatigue, C5 vault jam.
- Parallel work: P2/P3 split within Act I, P5/P6 split within Act II, P7/P8 split within Act III after recap.
- Final convergence: P9-P10 requires public state cards and three relics, not memory from 60 minutes earlier.
