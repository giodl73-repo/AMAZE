# Astral Archive Overnight Floorplan

## Envelope

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Trailer model/envelope | Premium mobile planning envelope; exact trailer or linked-module layout must be declared before build |
| Interior length | 20-28 ft planning range or two linked smaller modules |
| Interior width | 7-8 ft planning range |
| Interior height | 7 ft planning height |
| Door/threshold constraints | visible exit plus staff-controlled pause/comfort route |
| Power location | staff end low-voltage shelf |
| Operator location | staff end with sightline to act state board and final vault |
| Maximum team size | 6 players |

## Zone map

| Zone | Dimensions | Purpose | Player capacity | Key props/mechanisms | Staff access |
|---|---|---|---:|---|---|
| Z1 Entry and act board | 4 ft x full width | safety, roles, act progress, comfort-pause policy | 6 | visible exit, act board, role cards | direct |
| Z2 Map archive | 6 ft wall plus 2 ft bench | Act I star-map and catalog work | 4 | constellation drawers, map tiles, relic tray | aisle access |
| Z3 Instrument alcove | 6 ft wall band | Act II astrolabe/spectrum work | 4 | rotating astrolabe, filter cards, seated stool | side access |
| Z4 Memory table | 5 ft x 3 ft counter | Act III cipher/chronicle work | 4 | daybook panels, tactile index, rest surface | front access |
| Z5 Orrery vault | 5 ft x 4 ft end zone | final relic docking and dawn coordinate | 6 gathered | three relic sockets, vault wheel, star field | rear hatch |
| Z6 Egress/rest lane | full length clear lane | circulation, emergency exit, comfort pause | 6 moving | floor tape, no storage | always clear |

## Flow

| Moment | Player movement | Gathering point | Crowding risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Briefing | enter, see exit, learn 3-act structure | Z1 | doorway queue | move team to act board |
| Act I | split between drawers and map bench | Z2 | drawer crowd | two-lane drawer/map roles |
| Act transition 1 | relic docks to act board, optional sip/seat | Z1/Z3 | low | timed 2-minute decompression |
| Act II | players rotate to astrolabe and spectrum filters | Z3 | wheel crowd | caller/checker/handler |
| Act transition 2 | seated chronicle briefing | Z4 | low | rest surface and state recap |
| Act III | table synthesis plus final relic prep | Z4/Z5 | table crowd | state cards and role rotation |
| Finale | all gather at vault | Z5 | high | floor marks and operator cue |

## Egress and safety paths

| Path | Use | Player-visible? | Staff override | Blockers to prevent |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Normal exit | session end and comfort interruption | yes | operator opens/holds route | act bins, stools, or final crowd in lane |
| Emergency exit | immediate safety exit | yes | stop script, lights up, door path clear | loose map tiles or relics on floor |
| Staff access | hints, pause, reset, recoveries | partially | staff enters from egress lane/rear hatch | players leaning into vault hatch |

## Transport and mounting notes

| Item/zone | Mounting assumption | Travel risk | Secure/stow plan |
|---|---|---|---|
| Map archive drawers | shallow wall drawers or faux drawer board | drawer slide damage | travel latch and sleeve |
| Astrolabe wheel | removable mounted wheel | pointer bend | travel lock |
| Memory table panels | flat folding panels | edge wear | padded folio |
| Orrery vault | bolted end module | wheel/socket movement | hard cover and latch |
| Act bins | three labeled bins | reset mix-up | color-coded lids and photos |

## Open spatial risks

- 90-minute session may need a real comfort-break policy before paid use.
- Six-player final crowd could block sightlines without floor marks.
- Reset bins and stool must not drift into the egress lane.
