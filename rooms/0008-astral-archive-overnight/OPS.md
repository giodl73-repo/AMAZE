# Astral Archive Overnight Operations

| Role | Responsibility | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Primary operator | briefing, hints, act breaks, safety, comfort-pause decisions | required |
| Reset operator | 25-minute reset, act bins, component checks | strongly recommended for premium room |
| Floater/builder | vault/instrument repair and event turnover | recommended until build is stable |

## Hint protocol

| Level | Use when | Style | Example |
|---|---|---|---|
| Nudge | right station, no progress | state-board redirect | "Which act state does this object belong to?" |
| Point | wrong lane for 3+ minutes | physical pointer | point to route rail or state card |
| Demonstrate | slow max approaching | one false case | "This angle cannot open the second sky." |
| Accelerate | act-end clock pressure | grant first coordinate/date/token | staff marks earned state |
| Bypass | component failure or safety need | manual relic/vault card | story-preserving archive override |
| Pause | fatigue/restroom/medical need | stop or hold state per policy | escort and preserve board |

## Multi-room operator rotation

| Scope | Coverage model | Max rooms | Safe when | Unsafe when | Handoff signal | Dedicated staff trigger |
|---|---|---:|---|---|---|---|
| Two premium trailers | roving primary plus camera/audio wall | 2 | both rooms are between act breaks, cameras show egress, and state boards are visible | comfort pause, vault/finale active, reset in progress, or any safety/accessibility concern | room state light plus act-board timestamp | any restroom/medical pause, C5 vault jam, or simultaneous finale |
| Three standard/lite rooms | central operator with one reset floater | 3 | rooms are staggered by at least 10 minutes and no C5 finale is active in more than one room | overlapping intros/finales, low visibility, active transport/setup, or group with known access need | green/yellow/red room dashboard and radio check | anxious team, child-heavy group, accessibility support request, or manual bypass |
| Escape-room building cluster | control-desk operator plus hall runner | 3 | doors, cameras, emergency stops, and hint channels are continuously monitored | any room lacks camera/audio, egress blind spot exists, or hall runner is unavailable | desk log with room phase and next intervention time | fire/egress alarm, player distress, stuck door, or room evacuation |

## Failure modes

| Failure | Detection | Player-facing script | Staff action | Post-run fix |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Fatigue drift | silence, sitting on floor, slow movement | "The night watch rotates now." | watch-change pause and role rotation | adjust pacing |
| Restroom/medical interruption | player request or operator concern | "The archive preserves your place." | preserve state, escort, resume or exit | log policy result |
| State card lost | board mismatch | "The archive keeps a duplicate record." | duplicate act pack | improve dock |
| Drawer/tile jam | tile/card stuck | "The clerk has a duplicate catalog slip." | staff coordinate/tile | repair drawer |
| Filter glare | players avoid filters | "Use the high-contrast house marks." | work light/printed card | matte update |
| Vault jam | relic/wheel stuck | "The archive accepts the verified dawn record." | manual vault card and exit token | tolerance/guard fix |
