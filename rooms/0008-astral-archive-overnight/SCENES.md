# Astral Archive Overnight Scenes

## Party assumptions

| Team archetype | Personas/roles | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Amazing team | systems thinker, map handler, wheel handler, story keeper, caller | May enjoy depth but skip decompression unless invited. |
| Confused team | careful reader, hesitant handler, quiet observer, role follower | Needs act board and state cards to prevent overload. |
| Fighting team | dominant caller, ignored map handler, skeptical checker, tired player | Needs role rotation and final caller/checker/handler ritual. |
| Chaotic team | drawer opener, wheel spinner, relic grabber, speed guesser | Needs no-guess locks, durable relics, and operator acceleration. |
| Accessibility-varied team | seated story keeper, low-light-sensitive player, companion handler | Needs seating, work-light, non-color backup, and low reach. |
| Operator stress team | late group, fatigued group, reset-pressure staff | Tests pause, hint, reset, and manual bypass scripts. |

## Session timing model

| Timing field | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Target min | Planned puzzle-beat total is 69 minutes, leaving 21 minutes for act transitions, comfort pauses, story payoff, and variance. |
| Hint at min | Operator intervenes earlier than normal when fatigue, memory overload, or one-player dominance appears. |
| Slow max min | Slow-case beat ceiling is 88 minutes, so paid use still needs act-level accelerators and hard exit policy. |
