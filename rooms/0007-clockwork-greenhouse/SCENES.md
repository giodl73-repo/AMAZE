# Clockwork Greenhouse Scenes

## Party assumptions

| Team archetype | Personas/roles | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Amazing team | systems thinker, tray sorter, valve handler, caller | May solve quickly and want optional plant lore. |
| Confused team | careful reader, hesitant handler, quiet observer | Needs the four-system frame made obvious. |
| Fighting team | dominant caller, ignored tray handler, skeptical checker | Needs final caller/checker/handler ritual. |
| Chaotic team | token grabber, valve spinner, speed guesser | Needs no-guess sockets and durable trays. |
| Accessibility-varied team | seated caller, low-light-sensitive player, companion handler | Must solve without scent, darkness, crawling, or high reach. |
| Operator stress team | late group, tired operator, reset pressure | Operator must track piece counts and proof states. |

| Path | Beats | Unlocks | Fast coherence | Slow coherence | Operator acceleration |

## Session timing model

| Timing field | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Target min | Planned puzzle-beat total is 30 minutes inside a 45-minute staffed-hour slot. |
| Hint at min | Operator intervenes before sorting or alignment becomes private/random. |
| Slow max min | Slow-case ceiling is 40 minutes before acceleration or manual proof release. |

| prism glare | offer work light | "Use the greenhouse inspection light." | printed light token card |
| final takeover | assign roles | "One gardener calls, one checks, one turns." | staff verifies tokens |
| wheel jam | manual bloom release | "The conservatory accepts the verified bloom record." | route to exit token |
