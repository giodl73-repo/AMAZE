# Clockwork Greenhouse Floorplan

## Envelope

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Trailer model/envelope | Planning envelope only: 7 ft x 16 ft interior mobile trailer |
| Interior length | 16 ft planning length |
| Interior width | 7 ft planning width |
| Interior height | 7 ft planning height |
| Door/threshold constraints | One visible entry/exit door; no puzzle blocks door swing |
| Power location | Staff end low-voltage shelf if glow effect is used |
| Operator location | Staff end with sightline to central bench and bloom wheel |
| Maximum team size | 5 players |

## Zone map

| Zone | Dimensions | Purpose | Player capacity | Key props/mechanisms | Staff access |
|---|---|---|---:|---|---|
| Z1 Entry potting rail | 3 ft x 7 ft | briefing, safety, role frame | 5 | gardener log, visible exit, role tags | direct |
| Z2 Planting bench | 5 ft x 2 ft counter | seed/soil sorting and proof tray | 3 | seed trays, soil tiles, proof rail | front and side |
| Z3 Utility wall | 6 ft x 2 ft wall band | water valve and prism/light work | 3 | valve board, prism slider, shade cards | aisle access |
| Z4 Bloom wheel | 4 ft x 3 ft end panel | final token seating and flower reveal | 3 | four token sockets, wheel, manual release | rear service hatch |
| Z5 Clear aisle | 16 ft x 3 ft | circulation and egress | 5 moving | floor tape, no prop storage | always open |

## Zone-purpose allocation

| Zone/visible element | Gameplay purpose | Safety/ops purpose | Atmosphere purpose | Shared/reused? | Underused or overloaded risk |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Potting rail | names four systems | exit and no-force briefing | traveling greenhouse | shared | role tags could clutter |
| Planting bench | soil/seed proofs | keeps props contained | cozy workbench | shared | seed pieces could scatter |
| Utility wall | water/light proofs | spreads players | greenhouse controls | shared | valve board may crowd |
| Bloom wheel | final convergence | operator-visible proof state | clockwork flower payoff | shared | final bottleneck |
| Clear aisle | movement | emergency path | greenhouse walkway | no | must stay prop-free |

## Flow

| Moment | Player movement | Gathering point | Crowding risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Briefing | team enters, sees exit and potting rail | Z1 | doorway crowd | operator steps team into aisle |
| Split systems | players divide seed/soil and water/light work | Z2/Z3 | bench pileup | role tags: sorter, valve hand, light checker, caller |
| Proof rail | proofs collect at bench then move to bloom wheel | Z2/Z4 | token queue | rail has four silhouettes |
| Bloom | team gathers at wheel for final call | Z4 | high | caller/checker/handler ritual |
| Exit/reset | team exits; staff resets clockwise | Z5/all zones | reset cart in aisle | cart stays outside trailer |

## Egress and safety paths

| Path | Use | Player-visible? | Staff override | Blockers to prevent |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Normal exit | session end | yes | operator opens/holds door | no bins or chairs in aisle |
| Emergency exit | immediate stop | yes | stop script, lights up | loose seeds, trays, or role tags on floor |
| Staff access | hint/reset/recovery | partial | aisle entry and rear hatch | players leaning over service hatch |

## Operator sightlines

|---|---|---|---|---|

| P5 bloom wheel | yes | token sockets face operator | crowding | caller/checker ritual |

## Accessibility and reach

| Need | Current path | Open question |
|---|---|---|
| Seated/limited-mobility participation | bench controls and bloom wheel between 30 and 48 inches; caller/checker role can solve without reaching all props | verify real aisle width |
| No crawling/climbing required | all props on bench or wall | none |
| Low-light alternative | high-contrast labels and work-light mode | test prism glare |
| Audio alternative | ticking and bloom sound are flavor only | none |
| Reach range for required mechanisms | valves, sliders, tokens mounted at counter height | test with cardboard wall |

## Reset paths

| Reset route | Staff action | Verification point | Time risk |
|---|---|---|---|
| Bench reset | sort seeds/soil tiles into silhouettes | reset photo and count card | tiny seed pieces |
| Utility wall reset | valves home, prism slider home, shade cards stacked | colored home marks | valve drift |
| Bloom wheel reset | remove tokens, close petals, set wheel home | operator mark hidden | pinch/jam |
| Final sweep | clear aisle and count pouches | inventory card | missing token |

## Transport and mounting notes

| Item/zone | Mounting assumption | Travel risk | Secure/stow plan |
|---|---|---|---|
| Seed trays | removable lidded tray | piece loss | numbered pouch and tray lid |
| Valve board | wall panel on cleats | pointer bend | travel lock |
| Prism panel | acrylic slider in sleeve | scratch/glare | padded flat sleeve |
| Bloom wheel | bolted end panel | petal/wheel movement | latch closed and foam spacer |

## Open spatial risks

- Final bloom wheel may crowd if five players all want to see the reveal.
- Loose seed pieces could become a trip/reset risk without trays.
- Prism/light panel may glare under event lighting.
